**Tesla Releases Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2023 Financial Results**
======================================================================

January 24, 2024 04:07 PM Eastern Standard Time 

AUSTIN, Texas--([BUSINESS WIRE](https://www.businesswire.com/))--Tesla has released its financial results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2023 by posting an update on its Investor Relations website. Please visit [https://ir.tesla.com](https://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&url=https%3A%2F%2Fir.tesla.com&esheet=53887558&newsitemid=20240124529420&lan=en-US&anchor=https%3A%2F%2Fir.tesla.com&index=1&md5=0bfb9200cbfb55d9e0f8d8315e1eba58) to view the update.

As previously announced, Tesla management will host a live question and answer (Q&A) webcast at 4:30 p.m. Central Time (5:30 p.m. Eastern Time) to discuss the results and outlook.

**What:** Tesla Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2023 Financial Results Q&A Webcast  
**When:** Wednesday, January 24, 2024  
**Time:** 4:30 p.m. Central Time / 5:30 p.m. Eastern Time  
**Q4 & FY 23 Update:** [https://ir.tesla.com](https://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&url=https%3A%2F%2Fir.tesla.com&esheet=53887558&newsitemid=20240124529420&lan=en-US&anchor=https%3A%2F%2Fir.tesla.com&index=2&md5=47a32dd2a90bf6b989c023ff15076b23)  
**Webcast:** [https://ir.tesla.com](https://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&url=https%3A%2F%2Fir.tesla.com&esheet=53887558&newsitemid=20240124529420&lan=en-US&anchor=https%3A%2F%2Fir.tesla.com&index=3&md5=007d57bf37ba5afebc8712567d268871) (live and replay)

The webcast will be archived on the company’s website following the call.
